### 🌪️ The "Dam Break" Stress Test
[cite_start]To evaluate massive-scale granular fluid dynamics, the engine was subjected to a highly chaotic 1-million particle gravitational collapse simulation[cite: 967, 978]. 
* [cite_start]**Immunity to Structural Degradation:** As the simulation transitioned from complete spatial uniformity to widespread chaotic distribution, the engine successfully prevented catastrophic rebuild latency[cite: 1029, 1030].
* [cite_start]**Execution Flatline:** Despite the massive increase in spatial entropy, both the topology build time and the physics integration execution time remained strictly constant $O(1)$ throughout the entire simulation[cite: 1051].

### ⚖️ Absolute Thermodynamic Stability
Replicating real-world physics requires strict energy conservation. [cite_start]The engine was evaluated using a semi-implicit Symplectic Euler integrator within a closed elastic collision system[cite: 1422].
* [cite_start]**Energy Conservation:** Tracked over a 1,000-frame window, the system demonstrated a physically plausible energy conservation ratio of 0.992x[cite: 1423, 1424].
* [cite_start]**Perfect Determinism:** The engine achieves perfect 100% bitwise determinism under fixed configurations, guaranteeing the exact same mathematical output on every execution[cite: 1427, 1428].
